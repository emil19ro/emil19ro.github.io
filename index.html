Introduction: A Passion for Technology

My passion for modern technologies has always been present, but it truly took off during the pandemic when I had more time to explore. As a dedicated player of an MMORPG, I discovered an open-source emulator maintained by the community for over 13 years. Curious to learn more, I installed it and tried to understand the C++ code behind it. However, I quickly realized that I had no prior experience with the language.

My Journey into Programming

Determined to learn c++, I completed a 70-hour course on Udemy, which sparked a deep fascination with programming. After finishing the course, I started modifying the emulator, gradually becoming more confident in my skills. At some point, I had an idea: what if I could implement AI-controlled bots? Since some content required large groups of players, I wanted to create virtual companions to replace real players, allowing me to enjoy the game on my own terms. What began as a simple concept evolved into a four-year-long project, where I developed and refined intelligent bot behavior, enhancing the game’s experience.

Formal Education and Industry Experience

This project ignited my desire to pursue software development professionally. To formalize my knowledge, I enrolled in an intensive one-year coding school, attending 40 hours of in-person classes per week (Monday to Friday, 8 hours per day). I studied Python, Java, Kivy, Spring Boot, and Angular, successfully passed all my exams, and obtained my diploma. Following this, I completed a six-month internship at a software company, gaining hands-on industry experience.

Real-World Projects and Development Expertise

Beyond my studies, I have worked on multiple projects for friends, family, and clients, developing custom applications using Angular and ASP.NET. These experiences have helped me refine my expertise in both front-end and back-end development while delivering real-world solutions. While I have a strong preference for ASP.NET and Angular due to their scalability and efficiency, I adapt to the needs of each project and use the right language or framework whenever necessary. I have never hesitated to quickly learn new technologies such as Lua, Python, XML, or any other tool required to achieve my goals efficiently.

Lifelong Learning and Growth

To this day, I am continuously learning and exploring new frameworks and tools, driven by a deep curiosity and a commitment to writing efficient, maintainable, and high-quality code.
